# Disclaimer

This site is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or otherwise connected to the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign** or its Career Center. It is an independent re-presentation of publicly available UIUC Career Center CPT/OPT reports, built to help international students explore historical hiring data.

## Data

The underlying data comes from spreadsheets published by the UIUC Career Center. Each row in the source data represents one CPT or OPT work-authorization event for a UIUC student during the reported term — not necessarily a current open role, a job offer, or an ongoing sponsorship arrangement.

## Accuracy

- Employer names in the source data are inconsistent (whitespace, legal-entity variants, subsidiaries). We apply rule-based merges (see `data/aliases.yml`) but **errors are inevitable**. If you spot a wrong merge or a missing alias, please file an issue.
- A company appearing here is **not a guarantee** that it currently sponsors visas, hires international students, or even has open roles. Hiring policies, visa-sponsorship policies, and corporate structures change frequently.
- Always **verify directly with the employer** before applying, especially regarding sponsorship eligibility.
